2019 Q2

This is when TRTG was launched. We launched the mainnet, released the Windows and Linux wallets. We were listed on FCB. The rest of the quarter will be spent getting on more exchanges. We will release a tipbot. We will also hopefully release a MacOS wallet during this time. We will also try to be listed on Coinmarketcap, Coingecko and Discordtip.

2019 Q3

By this time, hopefully we will have a payment platform similar to TurtlePay, so vendors can accept TRTG easily. We will also be listed on more exchanges. We will also create mobile wallets for iOS and Android. GUI wallets for Windows, MacOS and Linux will be released.

2019 Q4

We will be hopefully used similar to regular currency. We will be listed on many more exchanges. We will hopefully partner with credit card or debit card companies to create a TRTG Card that would work similar to the Bitpay card that is currently used with bitcoin.
